Terrarium maintenance:

1- Water your glass garden when the soil is dry.

2- If you keep the terrarium closed , leave the door open for 1-2 hours a day so that fresh air flows in the terrarium and prevent mold and fungus.

3- Never place the closed terrarium directly in front of the sunlight.

4- Clean the inside of your terrarium once a month. If the glasses are dirty, the plants cannot receive the light and stay healthy.

5- If you see a sick and damaged plant, separate it from the others so that your entire terrarium is not affected.
